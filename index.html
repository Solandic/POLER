How To Buy

Download MetaMask

Install the MetaMask browser extension or mobile app to create a digital wallet for securely storing your cryptocurrencies and interacting with blockchain applications.

Secure Solana (BASE ETH)

Purchase Solanachain Ethereum (BASE), the foundational currency required for transactions on the base network where $POLERO operates. BASE ETH acts as the "gas" for transactions, including buying $POLERO.

Buy $POLERO

Use your newly acquired BASE ETH to purchase $POLERO tokens through a cryptocurrency exchange or decentralized exchange platform that lists $POLERO.
